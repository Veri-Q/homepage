I am Ji Guan, an **[Associate Professor](https://people.ucas.ac.cn/~guanji)** at the Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences.

I received my bachelor's degree in **Computational Mathematics** from **Sichuan University** in 2014, and my Ph.D. in **Quantum Computing** from the **Centre for Quantum Software and Information**, **University of Technology Sydney**, in 2018. During my doctoral study, I was supervised by **Prof. Mingsheng Ying** and **Prof. Yuan Feng**.

My research focuses on **formal methods for quantum computing**, especially the security, privacy, correctness, and reliability of quantum algorithms, quantum programs, and noisy quantum systems.

I am also leading the group of **FM4QC**: *Formal Methods for Quantum Computing*.

---

## Research Interests
- Formal verification and model checking of quantum systems
- Trustworthy quantum machine learning
- Quantum privacy and differential privacy
- Analysis and optimization of noisy quantum circuits

## Highlights
